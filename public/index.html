Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What brands of makeup do you use? +
For luxury bridal and HD makeup, I use top-tier international brands including MAC, Huda Beauty, NARS, Charlotte Tilbury, and Estée Lauder to ensure long-lasting, flawless results.
Do you travel to the venue? +
Yes, absolutely! I am available to travel to your venue within Mumbai, and for destination weddings globally. Additional travel and accommodation charges may apply for outstation bookings.
How early should I book for my wedding? +
It is recommended to book at least 3 to 6 months in advance, especially during peak wedding seasons, to secure your preferred dates and schedule a trial session if needed.
Do you provide group packages? +
Yes, I provide curated group packages for bridesmaids and family members. Please contact me directly via WhatsApp to discuss group pricing and custom requirements.
